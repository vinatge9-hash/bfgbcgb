About

A small roastery with big respect for craft, community, and responsible sourcing.

Our Story

Founded by coffee obsessives and hospitality veterans, Aurora & Bean began as a single roastery with a clear mission: make every cup feel like a small luxury. We roast in small batches, measure precisely, and partner directly with growers to ensure quality and transparency at every step.

2016
Founding — a tiny roastery and a big idea: consistency through craft.
2019
Opened our first café and began direct-trade partnerships with producers in Central America.
2023
Launched our subscription program and microlot releases — offering consistency and discovery to our members.

Direct Trade

We pay premiums, visit partners, and publish producer profiles for traceability.

Sustainable Packaging

Compostable bags on select products and recyclable mailers for subscription orders.

Community

We support local coffee education and host monthly cuppings open to the public.
